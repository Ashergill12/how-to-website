How to Box

Boxing is a combat sport where you fight in a ring with your hands only.
When your fighting in boxing you wear gloves and under those gloves you wear handwraps and you also wear a mouth gaurd.
When there is a boxing fight it normally has 3-5 rounds that are 3-5 minutes long. with a referee in the ring overseeing the fight.

There are 3 ways to win:
    A knockout
    A technical knockout
    A decision

A knockout is when an oppenent gets hit by a punch or many that causes them to fall and be unable to get up in under a 10 second count by a referee

A technical knockout is when an oppenent gets injured in that fight in a legal way that causes the ref to stop the fight.

A decision is when the fight ends and the winner is decided by the judges who keep a score for each round based on punches landed
