Defence

In boxing defence works in a lot of different ways.
  1. Dodging
  2. Blocking
  3. And countering


Dodging


In dodging the point is to react and completely avoid an oppnenets punch.
And there are lots of ways to do this.
  1. Slipping: when you bend down to either your left your right taking your head of the center line of your oppnent
  2. Rolling: Changing your head level by going up and down in the shape of a U to get under you oppnents punch
  3. Pivot: When you turn a different direction
  4. Step back: When you keep you front foot on the ground but take a step back with your rear leg and then lean backwards


Blocking

  1. High gaurd: It is when you keep your hands on your forehead to block your face from any punches.
  2. Elbow Block: Is to block body shots you do it by leaning and crunching the side the punch to going towards so you elbow gets in the way but you dont want to lower your arms.
  3. Hook block: Is like when you run a comb through the side of your hair, you do the same motion with your hand which covers the side of your heading blocking hooks.


Countering

  1. Catch: When your turn your hand to where your palm is facing the incoming punch and just push the punch a liitle. You don't want to make a big swing at the punch just a little push back at them to interupt the force of the punch while you other hand is open to punch back.
  2. Parry: When you put your palm over the top of there hand and push it down when they punch leaving half there face open for your other hand to punch.
  3. Forearm lift: When your oppnent punches you want to get your forarm and elbow under it and lift it up to leave there body exposed.
